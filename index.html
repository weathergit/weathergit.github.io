关于我 About Me

我于2025年获得西北农林科技大学博士学位，现为中国农业大学博士后研究员，合作导师为王喜庆教授。我的研究聚焦于农业生态系统与作物模型、遥感与GIS、计算机视觉与图像处理（表型组学）、以及机器学习与数据科学的交叉领域。我擅长使用Python和R进行数据分析和模型开发。

I received my PhD from Northwest A&F University in 2025 and am currently a postdoctoral researcher at China Agricultural University, working with Prof. Wang Xiqing. My research focuses on the intersection of agricultural ecosystems & crop modeling, remote sensing & GIS, computer vision & image processing (phenotyping), and machine learning & data science. I am proficient in Python and R for data analysis and model development.
